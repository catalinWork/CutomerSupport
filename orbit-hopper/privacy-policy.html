Orbit Hopper

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Overview

Orbit Hopper is a free mobile arcade game developed by Catalin Lazar. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what data is collected, how it's used, and your choices.

Orbit Hopper does not require an account, does not collect personal information directly, and does not store any data on external servers controlled by the developer.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted:

  • High score and game statistics
  • Sound, music, and haptic preferences

This data is cleared if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Orbit Hopper uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob serves ads and may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and approximate location to deliver relevant ads. Rewarded ads are always optional.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)

Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as session duration, screen views, and device information to help improve the game. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Firebase privacy info: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Children's Privacy

Orbit Hopper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Your Choices

  • Ad tracking: You can limit ad personalization through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Data deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
